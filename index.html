Students will prepare and deliver an individual presentation (8–15 slides) on a topic they are personally passionate about. Presentations must be submitted through Moodle according to the class schedule. Each project will be evaluated based on the quality of the in-class presentation, including content relevance and accuracy, depth of analysis, and completeness. Organization, clarity, structure, and timing will also be assessed. Visual aids should be professionally designed and support the key points, while delivery will be judged on engagement, confidence, and communication skills. Students will answer questions during a brief Q&A to demonstrate understanding of the topic. Professional appearance and respectful behavior are required. Presentations should be creative, clearly structured, and not read verbatim from notes. PowerPoint or other appropriate technology is expected.
